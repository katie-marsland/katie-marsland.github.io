About

Katie Marsland

Hello, and welcome! My name is Katie. I'm a Vancouver-born, Ottawa-raised, Toronto-based marketing specialist, graphic designer, and front-end web developer. I graduated in 2020 from the University of Toronto with my Hons. Bachelor of Arts, double majoring in Cinema Studies & Book and Media Studies. Growing up, my mother owned the only independent children's bookstore in Ottawa, which is where my love of storytelling began. This love of stories is also how I ended up in this field: communications and marketing are another way to tell stories, and to understand the experiences of people from all around the world. Outside of communications, I love to read and watch movies, bake and cook, knit and embroider, spend time with friends and family, and stare longingly at stranger's dogs that I see on the street. Send me a message via my contact page, I'd love to meet you!
